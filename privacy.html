Privacy Policy

Effective date: 30 Aug 2025

Snake.io is a casual web-based game (Progressive Web App). It runs entirely in your browser and can be installed to your device as an app. This policy explains what data we collect, use, and share.

1. What we collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. The game does not require an account, login, email, phone number, or permissions. Gameplay state (e.g., best score, settings like sound/vibration or emoji selection) may be saved locally on your device using standard browser storage (e.g., localStorage) so your preferences persist.

2. How the game works offline

Snake.io uses a Service Worker to cache essential game files (HTML, JS, icons) so the game can load offline. No network requests to external services are required for gameplay.

3. Cookies and third-party services

  • No third-party analytics, ads, or trackers are integrated.
  • No third-party SDKs are used.

4. Children’s privacy

The game is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete any such information from local project assets if applicable.

5. Data security

The web version is served over HTTPS. Because we do not collect personal data, there is no server-side user database. Local preferences remain on your device unless you clear your browser data.

6. Your choices

  • You can clear game data at any time by deleting site data in your browser settings (cache, storage).
  • On installed PWA, uninstalling the app removes its local data.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Effective date”.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at: youremail@example.com.
